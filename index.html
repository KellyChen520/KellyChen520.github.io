My Projects

Optimization
Flight Path Optimization

Design the algorithm to find the flight path with minimum cost under fuel regulations and threats from the enemy. Given information about the attack range and power of the threat points and the position coordinates of starting point, end pont, and threat points, the program generates the turning points of the best flight path.

Regression Analysis
Voter Turnout Analysis

Analyze the relationship between voter turnout in Taiwan and different factors, such as temperature, unemployment rate, and household income. Use data from 4 elections in 22 counties and cities and conclude that those factors have little influence on the election results.

Create a tourism website that enables users to sign up, log in, and order items. The database records information about users and their orders in the shopping carts. Every time the users log in, they can continue to add or delete products in the shopping carts.

Machine Learning

Deep Learning
